Pie and a computer part.

Today was our second day of ACS, or first day for Computer Science language.

The lecture was pretty great, a little boring cause it's a lecture, but very intriguing.

The picture that was shown today in class, of a piece of pie and a computer part, probably mean that a computer is like pie.

Hello World!

1/20/16

Us, the students, playing the balloon game.

Today was the first day of Accelerated Computer Science elective course. I was pretty excited when I walked into the room of learning, learn coding to be more specific.

I am writing this blog for 2 reason. One is because my teacher, the awesome Captain/Mr. Yoho, told us to do this so we can get used to writing day by day blog entries to remind us of the work we did. The second reason is because I want to learn the experience of write day by day entries, whether blog or daries.

What I hope to gain out of this is just to remember moments in high school, since I kind of have a bad memory, and the skill of day by day writing.

The elective I am taking, Accelerated Computer Science, is about coding and using these coding skills to solve real world problems, but this class teaches us more than just simple coding. I took this class because of two reason. Firstly because my school required that I changed electives and secondly because I wanted to learn more about coding than I might not learn this year in computer science.

What I hope to get out of this class is more advanced coding skills and/or strategies that will help me later on in school and/or life.
