Challenge : Design a Band

Challenge Info

---Design thinking is a type of thinking strategy that is involves more
imagination and exploring than programmatic thinking. We are
studying this because we need this kind of thinking to be more
creative when doing stuff, meaining that we will be smart enough to make a prototype.

---There are 4 steps to the DEEP thinking process:
Discover
Empathy
Experiment
Produce

---We followed these steps to make our own albim cover. We
first discovered subjects to make our band names and album title.
We then used empathy to chose how our album would lookm like,
which was done by surveying our classmates. We then experimented
by showing the prototypes of our albums and chose the winner of the 2.
Finally, we are producing by putting our final work here.
